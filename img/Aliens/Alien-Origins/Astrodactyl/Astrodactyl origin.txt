Home-World : Terradino

Appearance
Astrodactyl is a terracotta and yellow pterosaur-like alien. He has 2 antennae. He has glider-like wings attatched to a jet pack-like structure on his back. He wears a black and green suit and green gloves. The Omnitrix is on his left shoulder.

Powers and Abilities
Astrodactyl can fly pretty fast with his jetpack and retractable wings
He has some kind of internalized star power that he can use to create energy whips
He can also fire a propulsion blast from his jetpack
He can fire energy blasts from his mouth
He can survive in space
